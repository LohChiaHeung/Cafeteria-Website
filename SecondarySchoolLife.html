This is actually a school study trip to Kuala Lumpur. (Lawatan Sambil Belajar) It is 2day1night trip. We went to Xiamen University, Sunway University, and also Asia Pacific University, and go to the I-City in Kuala Lumpur. It is a memorable experience for me because it is a golden oppurtunity that I can enjoy trip with my friends because that year is the last year of my secondary school life, year of 2019. We also no have much time to go out for meeting, because that year we need to study hard for our SPM, Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia.

This is some of the pictures that I attend school activities in my secondary school such as "Hari Guru", "Jamuan Hari Raya" and so on.
In teachers' day, we perfrom a kpop song which is "Kill this love" from Kpop Female Group which is "BlackPink". This is actually a happy memories because it is the first time dancing for most of us. The black shirt is our class uniform. The front side is our class name, 5SN. For the back side, is consisting of our names included our teacher, Puan Noor Jannah, our Physics teacher.
In order to encourage us to fight for SPM, SMK Mutiara Rini also organized activity which is "Kempen Teknik Menjawab Soalan" in school. We need to stay at school for 3days2night. This is also my first time sleeping in school. The weather in my school is really hot.XDD
