About Us

Our Story

We are featured traditional French pastry and coffee house with a dessert bar such as mille crepe, food and gallery space for art and design piece. We will make its best effort to create a unique place where customers can socialize with each other in a comfortable and relaxing environment while enjoying the best-brewed coffee,awesome food, finger food, desserts and course the best Mille Crepe in the world.

Our Objective

We will be in the business of presenting the most traditional French pastry to our customers. At the same time, we will also make our effort to cultivate the coffee culture in Johor Bahru, with the great ambience, convenient location, friendly customer service, and products of consistently high quality served in the café.

Our Services

We provided delivery service. Delivery app that we accepted is Foodpanda, GrabFood, Deliveroo, GrabHub and Justeat.
